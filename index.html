MIT Lincoln Laboratory

In Summer 2019, I accepted a position as a Software Engineering Intern at MIT Lincoln Laboratory

I worked with three other interns on the Calibration of Infrared Cameras for Lasercom Evaluation (CIRCLE)

The creation of CIRCLE reduces the time needed to calibrate lasercom terminals (which send and recieve laser beams to transmit data)

Instead of running a series of calibration tests over a few weeks, CIRCLE allows optical engineers at MIT-LL to run a full test suite overnight, without manually touching hardware anymore

CIRCLE benefits NASA and the U.S. Air Force in transmitting data at the speed of light with a much smaller risk of enemy jamming, such as occurs in radar
