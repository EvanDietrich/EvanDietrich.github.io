Evan Dietrich – MIT

Technical Researcher
2019 - 2021

CIRCLE: Calibration of Infrared Cameras for Lasercom Evaluation
  • Designed and developed an automated system that runs calibration tests on laser-communication terminals, capable of sending and receiving data across any atmospheric medium at light-speed.
  • Took the runtime of a calibration test series from 2-3 weeks down to overnight completion.
ENVOY: Enabling Natural-language Versatility & Opportunity
  • A comprehensive system to improve the machine translation of ASL with a design informed by Signing communities.
  • Computer vision captures users’ movements and facial features in real-time. Feature extraction and natural language processing enabled language modeling, for the conversion of ASL to English translation.
