Spatial Cognition Laboratory

Overview

After getting a sense of CS research and concluding my project at the HRI Lab, I joined Tufts' Spatial Cognition Lab, assisting in research on the connection between individual differences in spatial thinking and STEM learning, using those connections to develop assessments and interventions.

Here, I self-taught R and STATA to execute statistical analysis, conducted robust literature reviews, and wrote extensive manuscript.

Background

On any given day, we encounter myriad situations to which spatial visualization skills are key to successful completion of activity. In numerous occupations, STEM fields especially, a high level of spatial ability is not only beneficial, but indispensable for task completion.

It is our belief that STEM fields may be strengthened by a generation of children receiving an increased degree of visual-spatial teaching in their early education.
