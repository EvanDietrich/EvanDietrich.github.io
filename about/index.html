About

Biography

When I first decided to major in CS, I was fascinated by opportunities to automate powerful computation and deliver tangible results. For this reason, I spent significant time on year-round work experiences with visible impacts, quickly confirming my interest towards a career in SWE.

These practical applications developed my appreciation for the vast complexity inherent in real-world environments. To this end, I took grad courses in ML and added my second major, honing areas that would let me account for the subtleties and nuances that practical applications demand.

I recently finished undergrad, after 4 years in Greater Boston. I was drawn to study at Tufts because of its world-class research and interdisciplinary academics, as well as for the exceptional number of tech opportunities present in Boston.

I look forward to employing my knowledge and skills on specialized technical and quantitative teams in finance.
